Starting My 2D Animation Journey

My journey into 2D animation started with a simple curiosity: how can drawings move and make people feel something? At first, I was only interested in motion graphics, but gradually I fell in love with character animation and storytelling.

Animation is not just about movement. It is about emotion, timing, acting and storytelling. A good animation can make people laugh, cry, or even change the way they see the world.

My goal is not only to become an animator, but to become a director who tells meaningful stories through animation.
